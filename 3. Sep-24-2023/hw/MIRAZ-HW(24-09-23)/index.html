Event design and event planning work hand-in-hand to deliver a successful event. Events by Knight offer tailor made events. This ineans we incorporate both event design and event planning to create that perfect event.

The amount of time needed to plan an event will vary depending on the size and complexity of the event. However, it is generally a good idea to start planning at least 6-12 months in advance. This will give you enough time to secure a venue, book vendors, and promote the event.
